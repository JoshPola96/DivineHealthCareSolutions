Welcome To Divine HealthCare Solutions

We are a team of dedicated healthcare professionals committed to make a positive impact in your lives.

Why Choose Divine HealthCare Solutions?

At Divine Health Care Solutions, we believe in providing tailored care that meets the unique needs and preferences of each individual. Our person-centered approach ensures that every client receives support that is designed specifically for them, promoting health, independence, and well-being.

Learn More

Experienced Professionals

Our team consists of Registered Nurses, Registered Psychiatric Nurses, Disability Support Workers, and Personal Care Workers. With extensive experience in managing a wide range of disabilities and conditions, our staff is dedicated to delivering high-quality care.

Diverse Range of Services

We offer a variety of services, including:

  • Respite Care
  • Household and Daily Living Support
  • Social and Community Engagement
  • Group and Centre-Based Activities
  • Travel and Transport Assistance
  • Community Nursing Services

Commitment to Quality and Safety

We prioritize the health, safety, and well-being of our clients by adhering to best practices and regulatory standards. Our commitment to continuous improvement ensures that we are always enhancing our service delivery based on feedback from clients, families, and stakeholders.

Services

Respite and Home Care Services

Respite Care: Providing temporary relief for primary caregivers in participants’ own environment or respite accommodation.

Home Care Services: Offering assistance with daily living activities (ADLs) from low to high care.

Social and Community Engagement

Social Activities: Organizing indoor and outdoor activities to promote socialization and community engagement.

Innovative Community Participation: Encouraging community involvement through innovative programs.

Group and Center-Based Activities

Group and Centre-Based Activities: Facilitating group interactions and activities in center-based settings.

Transportation Services

Assist Travel/Transport: Providing transportation services for appointments and social events.

Household and Daily Living Support

Assistive Products for Household Activities: Providing assistance with daily living activities (ADLs) and household tasks using assistive products, tailored to meet individual needs from low to high care..

Community Nursing Services

Community Nursing: Offering comprehensive nursing care, including wound and medication management, enteral feeding, health assessments, risk evaluations, mental health support, healthcare referrals, and behavior management education.
